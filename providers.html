Healthcare Providers

Find informed consent clinics, endocrinologists, and other healthcare providers in your area.

Planned Parenthood

Location:Multiple locations nationwide

Type:In-person

Website:plannedparenthood.org

Notes:Many Planned Parenthood locations offer gender-affirming HRT services. Check your local clinic for availability and informed consent options.

Plume

Location:Statewide via telehealth

Type:Telehealth

Website:getplume.co

Notes:18+ only; gender-affirming HRT; subscription-based. Available in 37 states. Both estrogen and testosterone available in most states, estrogen only in some states. Minimum age 19 in Alabama and Nebraska.

QMed (QueerMed)

Location:Statewide via telehealth

Type:Telehealth

Website:queermed.com

Notes:Telehealth HRT for all ages; available in 47 states. Based in Atlanta, GA. Texas requires 18+.

QueerDoc

Location:Statewide via telehealth

Type:Telehealth

Website:queerdoc.com

Notes:Queer and trans healthcare providers. Adult (18+) and adolescent care available. Sliding scale payments. Available in Alaska, California, Florida, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Utah, Washington, and Wyoming.

Folx Health

Location:Statewide via telehealth

Type:Telehealth

Website:folxhealth.com

Notes:18+; virtual gender-affirming care including estrogen and anti-androgens. Available in all 50 states and DC. Labs and medication delivery via partner pharmacy or your choice. Insurance coverage varies by state and plan type.

Howard Brown Health

Location:Chicago (multiple clinics)

Type:In-person

Website:howardbrown.org

Notes:LGBTQ+ focused healthcare provider

True U Clinic

Location:Statewide via telehealth

Type:Telehealth

Website:trueuclinic.com

Notes:Available in Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Delaware, Idaho, Illinois, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Maine, Maryland, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Montana, Nevada, New Hampshire, New Mexico, New York, Ohio, Oregon, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Texas, Utah, Virginia, Washington, and Wyoming. 18+ only. Offers hormone therapy, PrEP & STI testing. No subscriptions required.
